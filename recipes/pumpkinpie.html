Pumpkin Pie

This is a recipe to make a yummy pumpkin pie. This is the best desert you'll ever find and if you disagree well you're wrong so you better make this xqcL

Ingredients

  • 1 (15 ounce) can pumpkin
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 (9 inch) unbaked pie crust
