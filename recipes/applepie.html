Apple Pie

This is a recipe for a yummy apple pie. Isn't as good as pumpkin pie but it's still good tho xqcL

Ingredients

  • 1 9 inch double pie crust
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 3 tablespoons all purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown super
  • 8 granny smith apples (peeled, cored, sliced)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F
  2. Melt butter in sauce pan and stir in flour to form paste
  3. Add water and both white and brown sugars and bring to boil
  4. Reduce temperature and simmer
  5. Put bottom crust in pan and fill with apples
  6. Cover with lattice work crust
  7. Gently pour sugar and butter liquid over crust
  8. Bake 15 minutes in preheated oven
  9. Reduce temperature to 350 degrees F
  10. Continue baking for 35 to 40 minutes until apples are soft
