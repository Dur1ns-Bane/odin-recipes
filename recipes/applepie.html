Apple Pie

This is a recipe for a yummy apple pie. Isn't as good as pumpkin pie but it's still good tho xqcL

Ingredients

  • 1 9 inch double pie crust
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 3 tablespoons all purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown super
  • 8 granny smith apples (peeled, cored, sliced)
