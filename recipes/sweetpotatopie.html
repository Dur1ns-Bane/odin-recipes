Sweet Potato Pie

This is a recipe for sweet potato pie. Why even make this? There's already a pumpkin pie recipe this is just a worse version although it's still alright but you do you I guess xqcL

Ingredients

  • 1 (1 pound) sweet potato
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 (9 inch) unbaked pie crust

Directions

  • Boil sweet potato whole in skin for 40 to 50 minutes
  • Run cold water over swwet potato and remove skin
  • Break apart sweet potato in bowk, then add butter and mix well
  • Stir in sugar, milk, eggs, nutmeg, cinnamon, and vanilla. Beat with medium speed until smooth
  • Poor mixture into unbaked pie crust
  • Bake at 350 degrees F for 55 to 60 minutes
